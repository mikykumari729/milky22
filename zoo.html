ATAL BIHARI VAJPAYEE BIOLOGICAL ZOO,Karnataka

Bhagwan Birsa Biological Park, Ranchi is one of the most beautiful and progressive zoos in India having 104 ha of area of mildly undulating topography with natural dry deciduous Sal forests & water bodies in and around it. It provides a natural habitat to wild animals to livein peace and harmony with the nature. It is situated on the Ranchi-Ramgarah section of National Highway 33, on the outskirts of Ranchi and about 20kms from it.
